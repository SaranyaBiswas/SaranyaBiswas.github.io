About Me

I am Saranya Biswas, a researcher in nonlinear stochastic dynamics. My academic journey started with a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from Jadavpur University, followed by an MS–PhD in Applied Mechanics from IIT Madras, where I worked on characterization of noisy dynamical systems. I completed my postdoctoral research at IIT Kanpur and currently serve as a Visiting Assistant Professor at Techno India University, Kolkata.

My research focuses on stochastic attractors, noise-induced bifurcations, computational dynamics, and stability analysis in nonlinear systems. I am passionate about translating theoretical insights into computational methods for complex systems.
