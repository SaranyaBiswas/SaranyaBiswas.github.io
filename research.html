Research Interests

Stochastic Dynamical Systems

Investigating noise-induced phenomena in nonlinear systems, including stochastic attractors and basin stability analysis.

Nonlinear Oscillations

Studying limit cycles, bifurcations, and intermittency in deterministic and stochastic oscillatory systems.

Computational Methods

Developing numerical simulations and algorithms to characterize noisy mechanical systems and complex dynamical models.
