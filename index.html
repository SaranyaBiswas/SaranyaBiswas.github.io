Saranya Biswas

Researcher in Nonlinear Stochastic Dynamics

I work on stochastic dynamical systems, nonlinear oscillations, noise-induced bifurcations, and stability of complex systems. My background includes an MS–PhD in Applied Mechanics from IIT Madras, followed by a postdoctoral position at IIT Kanpur. I am currently serving as a Visiting Assistant Professor at Techno India University, Kolkata.

Recent Work

My research focuses on characterizing stochastic attractors, basin stability, noise-induced intermittency, and computational methods for nonlinear mechanical systems. I collaborate on theoretical and numerical problems involving random perturbations and their effect on system stability and transitions.
