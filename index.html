Saranya Biswas

Researcher in Nonlinear Stochastic Dynamics

I work on stochastic dynamical systems, nonlinear oscillations, noise-induced bifurcations, and stability of complex systems. My background includes MS–PhD in Applied Mechanics from IIT Madras, postdoc at IIT Kanpur, and current Visiting Assistant Professor at Techno India University, Kolkata.

Recent Work

Stochastic Attractors & Noise-induced Bifurcations

Characterizing stochastic attractors, basin stability, noise-induced intermittency, and computational methods for nonlinear mechanical systems.

Computational Dynamics

Collaborating on theoretical and numerical problems involving random perturbations and their effects on system stability.
