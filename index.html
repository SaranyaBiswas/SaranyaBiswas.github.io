Saranya Biswas

Researcher in Stochastic Nonlinear Dynamics

I work on stochastic dynamical systems, nonlinear oscillations, noise-induced bifurcations, and stability of complex systems. My background includes an MS–PhD in Applied Mechanics from IIT Madras followed by postdoctoral research at IIT Kanpur.

Recent Works

Stochastic Attractors & Noise-induced Bifurcations

Characterizing stochastic attractors, basin stability, noise-induced intermittency, and computational methods for nonlinear mechanical systems.

Computational Dynamics

Working on theoretical and numerical analysis of nonlinear systems under random perturbations and uncertainty.

QP Software Platform for Teachers

Co-developer of QP, an open-source software based on MATLAB and LaTeX, designed for teachers handling large classes. QP automates question-paper generation, randomization, grading workflows, and exam management with high reliability.
