About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of nonlinear dynamics, stochastic processes, uncertainty quantification, and applied mechanics. My work focuses on understanding noisy dynamical systems through simulation, probabilistic modelling, and analytical techniques.

Research Interests

  • Nonlinear stochastic dynamics
  • Stochastic differential equations
  • Random vibration
  • Uncertainty quantification
  • Dynamical systems under noise
